About the Salon

For over 25 years, CHAMELEON Salon has served the most discerning clients in Limassol. Located near the Four Seasons Hotel in Agios Tychon, we combine European precision with genuine hospitality.

We work exclusively with professional European and American brands, ensuring every treatment delivers lasting, beautiful results. Quality and precision are not just values — they are our standard.
